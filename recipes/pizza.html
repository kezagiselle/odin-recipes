Pizza recipe

Description

Welcome to our pizza recipes, pizza is the most wanted and favourite dish someone can make each day

Recipe

  • Pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • Tomato sauce
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, olives
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Italian seasoning (optional)

    • Ingredients

    • 3 ½ cups all-purpose flour
    • 2 teaspoons instant yeast
    • 1 teaspoon sugar
    • 2 teaspoons salt
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil
    • 1 ½ cups warm water (around 110°F/43°C)

    Steps

    1. Prepare the dough:
      • In a large bowl, combine the flour, instant yeast, sugar
      • Make a well in the center of the dry ingredients and pour in the olive oil and warm water.
      • Stir the mixture until a dough forms.
      • Transfer the dough to a floured surface and knead for about 5-7 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic.
      • Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1-2 hours or until it doubles in size.
    2. Prepare the sauce:
      • In a bowl, combine the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, minced garlic, dried oregano,
      • Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly combined.
    3. Preheat the oven:
      • Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C) or the temperature recommended by your pizza dough recipe
    4. Shape the pizza:
      • Once the dough has risen, punch it down and transfer it to a floured surface.
      • Divide the dough into two equal portions if you want to make two pizzas, or keep it as one for a larger pizza.
      • Roll out each portion of dough into your desired pizza shape and thickness. You can use a rolling pin or stretch it by hand.
